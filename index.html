Why I love UNDERTALE:

The Characters:

Firstly, I love the characters. This game has some of the most loveable characters I've ever played in. Each character feels different and new, and you really don't know what to expect when talking to anyone. The skeleton you start fighting today might be your friend tomorrow. It's clear the game's developer Toby Fox put a "skele-ton" amount of work into it.

The Story:

The story is simply amazing. Every litle detail is so well thought out and super well written. It makes casual actions such as saving a basis for the entire story. Even naming your character is filled with jokes and future refrences.

The Gameplay:

This is one of the few RPG games I've seen you have a choice to fight or spare your opponents life. You can make friends with any opponent you fight, this makes for a really intresting play concept.

Some of my favorite characters:

Papyrus:

We first meet this skeleton in the snowy slopes of Snowdin and he is intent on capturing
a human in order to be a part of the Royal Guard so that he can finally be
popular and have friends.

Undyne:

Undyne is a fish-like monster that leads the Royal Guard. Clad in her full suit of
armor, she pursues the protagonist through the entirety of Waterfall a city
in the world of undertale

Nabstablook:

Nabstablook is a sad ghost that can be found in the RUINs and in Waterfall.
He's super nice and very intresting to hang out with. He also cries acid tears
and of course can't be killed.
